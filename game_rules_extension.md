2. Hand Capacity & Choice

2-Player Mode (The Duo): Because each player must cover the output of two limbs, they play 2 cards per Pulse. To compensate for the faster exhaustion, their Hand Capacity is 8 (instead of 5).

3-5 Players: Hand Capacity remains 5.

3. The Match Bonus Scaling (Suit Resonance)

To prevent the Golem from becoming "self-healing" with too many players, the refill rule is adjusted:

2-3 Players: One matching suit refills that player's hand.

4-5 Players: Two or more players must match the Terrain suit for the bonus to trigger. If only one player matches, they do not refill their hand on a failure.

4. Module Distribution

2 Players: Each player is assigned 2 Modules (Total 4).

3-5 Players: Each player is assigned 1 Module (Total 3, 4, or 5).

Note: In 5-player games, ensure all "Compulsory" parts are assigned first; remaining players choose from the "Optional" pool.
