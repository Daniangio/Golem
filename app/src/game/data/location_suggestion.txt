Sphere 9:
9.1: One of the player, which is the conductor, instead of playing normally, chooses how many cards to give to another player and performs the exchange before the pulse, and receives back the same amount of cards by that player (he does not play the pulse).
    compulsory faculties: ["conductor_of_streams", "triune_focus", "sophic_vent"]

9.2: At the start of each pulse, one card per player is randomly played from pulse deck. Then players play as usual (placing one card each). After the reveal, the value of all the played cards is summed as usual. All the cards that are played by one player that share the suit, have their values multiplied instead of summed.
    compulsory faculties: ["scales_of_geburah"]
    optional faculties: ["akashic_lens", "harmonized_memory", "sophic_vent"]

9.3: The total value of the played pulse cards is multiplied by the value of the card in the reservoir unless a Prism is played by any player.
    compulsory faculties: ["reinforced_hull"]
    optional faculties: ["akashic_lens", "harmonized_memory", "mercurial_joints"]

Sphere 10:
10.1: Player's hand is replaced by the terrain deck. Terrain cards are evenly distributed among players. Each time a player plays a terrain card from his hand, a pulse carde is randomly drawn from the deck and placed in his play zone, as if they where played by the player. All player's effects are applied to them. One player can play multiple times until three terrain cards are reveiled. The success is determined if the total value of pulse cards is within the range of at least one of the terrain cards played. A failure is treated as an overshoot.
10.2: Each time a player plays a pulse card from his hand, there is a 30% probability that the card is instead discarded and not played. If the card is discarded, the player must play another card from his hand. If the player is left with no cards in hand, the hand is fully replenished.
10.3: At the end of each pulse, the played pulse cards are not reveiled to the players and the effects of overshoot and undershoot to lives/friction are not applied. At the end of the last pulse, an overview of all the played pulse cards and relative terrain cards are shown to the players. Also, the score is updated using the following rule: each successful pulse adds one life, each overshoot or undershoot removes one life.
