CORE: Campaign Geography

The campaign consists of 10 Stages. Every Stage offers 3 branching paths (Locations). While each path in a Stage shares a theme (Level Deck), they offer different tactical challenges and rewards.

STAGE 1: The Awakening (Level Deck 1)

Common Theme: Low-intensity activation, teaching basic synergy.

Location 1.1: The Scrapheap Awakening

Compulsory (*): Static Core, Numb Leg.

Rule: The first Undershoot does not penalize the draw phase.

Reward: Heal $1$ HP.

Location 1.2: The Whispering Mire

Compulsory (*): Numb Leg, Muddled Sensors.

Rule: Players cannot refill hands if they play an Acid (Green) card.

Reward: Heal $1$ HP.

Location 1.3: The Shattered Ascent

Compulsory (*): Cracked Pistons, Heavy Chassis.

Rule: If no player matches the Terrain suit, add $+1$ Heat.

Reward: Heal $1$ HP + 1 Mulligan Token.

STAGE 2: The Rust-Belt Corridor (Level Deck 2)

Common Theme: Resource scarcity and suit-specific hazards. Ranges move toward $10-25$.

Location 2.1: The Foundry Ruins

Compulsory (*): Static Core, Overclocker.

Optional: Cooling Fan.

Rule: All Cinder (Red) cards count as $+2$ value, but using the Reservoir with a Red card costs $0$ Heat.

Reward: Heat Sink (Maximum Heat capacity increased to 4 for the next Stage).

Location 2.2: The Iron Graveyard

Compulsory (*): Numb Leg, Magnetic Grasp.

Optional: Fuse.

Rule: The Reservoir card is hidden. It is only revealed when a player chooses to perform a Swap.

Reward: Salvaged Gears (One player may permanently increase hand capacity by $+1$).

Location 2.3: The Tesla Wastes

Compulsory (*): Muddled Sensors, Static Core.

Optional: Sharp Eye.

Rule: If the total sum is an Even number, the Golem generates $+1$ Heat regardless of success.

Reward: Battery Pack (Gain 2 Mulligan Tokens).
